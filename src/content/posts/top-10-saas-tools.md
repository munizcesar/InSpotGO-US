## 1. HubSpot CRM — The center of your growth stack

HubSpot is the most popular CRM for SaaS that wants to align marketing and sales. It captures leads, runs email sequences, and syncs product usage for smarter outreach.

- **Best for:** Scaling SaaS companies that need a single source of truth for leads and revenue
- **Standout:** Free CRM with upgrade paths to powerful marketing automation
- **Watch out for:** Cost can grow quickly once you add multiple paid Hubs

## 4. Stripe — Billing + revenue analytics for subscriptions

Stripe is the de facto standard for recurring revenue payments. It handles billing, dunning, invoices, and revenue recognition, plus it offers a robust reporting API.

- **Best for:** SaaS product teams that want smooth checkout and subscription flows

## FAQ

### What is the most important tool for a SaaS launch?
For most early SaaS launches, a CRM (HubSpot or Pipedrive) plus a reliable billing platform (Stripe) are the minimum must-haves.

### Can I run a SaaS business without a CRM?
Technically yes, but you lose pipeline visibility. A CRM helps you track leads, manage follow-ups, and scale predictable sales.

### What tool should I choose first?
Pick the one that solves your biggest bottleneck: if you struggle to turn leads into trials, start with CRM/automation; if you struggle to keep customers, start with product analytics and support tools.
