## What to Consider When Choosing

**Battery Life:** Look for models with 5000mAh or more for heavy daily use.

**Display Quality:** AMOLED offers better contrast and colors compared to LCD.

**Camera Performance:** For better photos, aim for sensors above 48MP with good software processing.

**Software Updates:** Brands like Samsung and Xiaomi typically offer more consistent updates.

**5G Support:** Most budget phones now include 5G connectivity for future-proofing.

## Pro Tips

- Compare prices across different retailers and watch for seasonal sales
- Check for carrier deals that might offer additional discounts
- Consider refurbished flagships as alternatives to new budget phones
- Read user reviews for real-world battery and camera performance
