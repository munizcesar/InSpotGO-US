## Buying Considerations

### Display Quality
**AMOLED vs LCD:**
- AMOLED offers superior contrast, deeper blacks, and better outdoor visibility
- LCD provides adequate quality at lower cost
- 120Hz refresh rate now standard across budget segment

**Recommendation:** Prioritize AMOLED if media consumption is important (Xiaomi, Samsung, Realme)

### Battery Life Assessment
**Capacity Guidelines:**
- 5,000mAh: Standard for full-day usage
- 6,000mAh: Multi-day battery life (Motorola)
- Consider fast charging: 30W+ preferred

**Real-world Testing:**
Based on standardized battery tests (mixed usage, 50% brightness):
- Motorola Moto G Power: 48+ hours
- Xiaomi Redmi Note 13: 36 hours
- Samsung Galaxy A25: 34 hours
- Realme Narzo 60: 32 hours
- Nokia G400: 38 hours

### Camera Performance
**Daytime Photography:**
All tested devices produce acceptable photos in good lighting. Xiaomi and Samsung show better dynamic range.

**Low-Light Capability:**
- Xiaomi Redmi Note 13: Best with OIS
- Samsung Galaxy A25: Good with Night mode
- Others: Noticeable noise in low light

**Video Recording:**
Most support 1080p at 60fps; 4K limited to 30fps with basic stabilization.

### Software Updates
**Long-term Support:**
- Samsung: 4 OS + 5 security updates (best in class)
- Xiaomi: 2 OS + 3 security updates
- Motorola: 1 OS + 3 security updates
- Realme: 2 OS + 3 security updates
- Nokia: 2 OS + 3 security updates

**Recommendation:** Samsung Galaxy A25 offers best update commitment for long-term ownership.

### 5G Connectivity
All evaluated devices include 5G support. Verify band compatibility with your carrier before purchase.
