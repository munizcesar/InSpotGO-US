## What makes a marketing automation tool great for SaaS?

The best platforms for SaaS combine these capabilities:

- **Advanced email sequences** (drip, behavior-based, and time-based)
- **Lead scoring & segmentation** (especially by product usage)
- **Integration with CRM + product analytics** (so sales knows who is using the product)
- **Landing page/forms builders** (to capture and qualify leads fast)
- **Easy onboarding sequences** (to reduce churn during trial)

## How to choose the right tool

- Do you need built-in CRM or will you connect to an external CRM?
- Are your campaigns driven by product usage events (in-app actions)?
- How many contacts do you have, and what is your growth rate?
- Do you need multi-channel (SMS, push, chat) or just email?

## FAQ

### What is the best marketing automation tool for small SaaS teams?
For small teams, **ActiveCampaign** offers a powerful mix of email automation and CRM without needing a separate tool. **Mailchimp** is a simpler alternative for email-only workflows.

### Do I need a marketing automation tool if I use a CRM like HubSpot?
If your CRM includes marketing automation (like HubSpot), you may not need a separate tool. The advantage of dedicated marketing automation platforms is deeper email funnel control and lower cost for large lists.

### How do I start using marketing automation without losing control?
Start with one funnel: capture leads, send a welcome sequence, then trigger a nurture stream based on engagement. Use clear naming and separate automations for each funnel to avoid overlap.
