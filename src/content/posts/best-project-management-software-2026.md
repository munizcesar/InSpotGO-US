## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best project management software for small teams?
For small teams, Monday.com and Asana are excellent choices due to their intuitive interfaces and flexible free tiers.

### Which platform is best for Agile development?
Jira is purpose-built for Agile teams, offering advanced sprint planning and reporting features.

### Are there free project management tools?
Yes, most leading platforms offer free tiers with essential features suitable for individuals and small teams.

### How do I choose the right software for my business?
Consider your team size, workflow complexity, required integrations, and budget. Test free trials to evaluate fit before committing.

### Can these tools integrate with other business apps?
Yes, top platforms support integrations with popular tools like Slack, Google Workspace, and Microsoft 365, enhancing workflow automation.
