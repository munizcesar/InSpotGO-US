## Detailed Analysis

### MacBook Pro 16" M4 Pro (Best Overall)

**Specifications:**
- Processor: Apple M4 Pro (12-core CPU, 18-core GPU)
- RAM: 24GB unified memory (configurable to 96GB)
- Storage: 512GB SSD (up to 8TB)
- Display: 16.2" Liquid Retina XDR (3456×2234, 120Hz ProMotion)
- Battery: Up to 22 hours video playback
- Ports: 3× Thunderbolt 5, HDMI 2.1, SD card, MagSafe 3
- Weight: 4.7 lbs (2.15 kg)

**Performance Benchmarks:**
- Geekbench 6: Single 3,100 / Multi 22,500
- Cinebench R24: 1,850 (single) / 28,000 (multi)
- 4K video export (5-min): 2 minutes 15 seconds
- Battery life (real-world): 16-18 hours

**Strengths:**
- Exceptional performance with minimal fan noise
- Best-in-class display (1,600 nits peak HDR)
- Outstanding battery efficiency
- Premium build quality with aluminum unibody
- Excellent trackpad and keyboard
- Silent operation for most tasks

**Limitations:**
- Premium pricing tier
- macOS ecosystem only
- Limited gaming compatibility
- No touchscreen option

**Best for:** Video editors, developers, creative professionals invested in Apple ecosystem

**Price Category:** Premium

- Battery life: 8-10 hours (productivity)
- 4K video export: 3 minutes 30 seconds

**Strengths:**
- Gorgeous OLED display with deep blacks
- Powerful dedicated GPU for creative work
- Minimalist design aesthetic
- Thunderbolt 4 support
- Good Windows Hello face recognition

**Limitations:**

**Price Category:** Upper-mid
