## Key Considerations

### Processor Selection
**Intel Core Ultra (2026):** Good all-around performance, improved efficiency, AI features
**AMD Ryzen 8000:** Excellent multi-core performance, competitive pricing
**Apple M4/M4 Pro:** Best performance-per-watt, macOS only, excellent for creative work

### RAM Requirements
- **8GB:** Basic tasks only (not recommended for 2026)
- **16GB:** Good for most users, multitasking, light creative work
- **32GB:** Heavy multitasking, content creation, future-proofing
- **64GB+:** Professional video editing, 3D rendering, virtual machines

### Storage
- **256GB:** Minimum (tight for most users)
- **512GB:** Sweet spot for average users
- **1TB+:** Content creators, large game libraries
- **Prioritize SSD speed:** NVMe Gen 4 standard in 2026

### Display
**Resolution:**
- FHD (1920×1080): Adequate for 14" and smaller
- QHD+ (2560×1600): Good balance for 15-16"
- 4K: Best for creators, battery impact consideration

**Panel Type:**
- IPS: Good viewing angles, accurate colors
- OLED: Superior contrast, deeper blacks, richer colors
- Mini-LED: High brightness, good HDR (MacBook Pro)

**Refresh Rate:**
- 60Hz: Standard, adequate for productivity
- 120Hz: Smoother scrolling, better for creators
- 240Hz+: Gaming-focused

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: MacBook or Windows laptop?**
A: MacBook if you're in Apple ecosystem, need best battery life, or do video editing. Windows for gaming, more hardware options, broader software compatibility, or budget constraints.

**Q: How much RAM do I really need?**
A: 16GB for most users in 2026. Upgrade to 32GB if you do video editing, run virtual machines, or want to future-proof for 5+ years.

**Q: Is a dedicated GPU necessary?**
A: Not for productivity. Yes for gaming or professional 3D work. Integrated graphics (Intel Arc, Apple Silicon) now handle light photo/video editing.

**Q: What about battery life?**
A: Expect 8-10 hours for Windows productivity laptops, 15-20 hours for MacBooks, 4-6 hours for gaming laptops under normal use.

**Q: Should I wait for next generation?**
A: 2026 models offer significant improvements in AI features and efficiency. Unless a specific release is imminent (within 1-2 months), current models offer excellent value.
