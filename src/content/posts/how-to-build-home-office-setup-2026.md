## Room Layout & Environment

### Optimal Desk Positioning

**Option 1: Facing Wall (Focus)**
- Minimizes visual distractions
- Good for deep work
- Position 12-18 inches from wall
- Add art/inspiration board above monitor

**Option 2: Perpendicular to Window (Recommended)**
- Natural light from side (no glare)
- Maintains circadian rhythm
- Add sheer curtains for brightness control

**Option 3: Window View (Caution)**
- Pleasant but potential glare
- Monitor brightness must exceed outdoor light
- Use blackout shade for screen glare control

**Avoid: Back to Door**
- Creates subconscious stress
- Reduces focus
- If unavoidable, add mirror for peripheral awareness

### Climate Control

**Temperature:**
- Optimal range: 68-72°F (20-22°C)
- Each degree outside range reduces productivity 2-4%
- Smart thermostat: Nest Learning ($249)

**Humidity:**
- Target: 40-60% relative humidity
- Low humidity causes dry eyes, static
- High humidity promotes mold, discomfort
- Humidifier: Levoit LV600S ($90)
- Dehumidifier: hOmeLabs 4500 Sq Ft ($249)

**Air Quality:**
- HEPA air purifier for spaces >150 sq ft
- Coway Airmega 400: $450 (covers 1,560 sq ft)
- Change filters every 6-12 months
- Indoor plants: Snake plant, pothos (natural filtration)

### Acoustic Treatment

**For Video Calls and Focus:**
- Acoustic panels: TroyStudio (12-pack $40)
- Position on wall behind you (video background)
- Add bookshelf with books (natural sound absorption)
- Thick curtains reduce echo
- Area rug (50-70% floor coverage ideal)

**For Content Creation:**
- Professional foam panels: Auralex Studiofoam
- Bass traps in room corners
- Invest if recording podcasts/videos regularly

## Maintenance Schedule

### Daily
- Wipe desk surface
- Organize cables after unplugging devices
- Return items to designated spots

### Weekly
- Vacuum floor and chair
- Clean monitor with microfiber cloth
- Adjust desk height (alternate sitting/standing ratio)

### Monthly
- Deep clean keyboard and mouse (compressed air)
- Dust monitor stand and desk accessories
- Check cable connections
- Clean microphone windscreen

### Quarterly
- Lubricate standing desk mechanism
- Check chair adjustments and tighten bolts
- Replace air purifier pre-filter
- Evaluate equipment needs (upgrade planning)

### Annually
- Deep clean entire office (behind furniture)
- Replace HEPA filters
- Update software on all devices
- Assess ergonomic setup (schedule physical if back/wrist pain)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Ergonomic Errors
- ❌ Monitor too low (causes neck strain)
- ❌ Chair arms too high (shoulder tension)
- ❌ Keyboard too far (overreaching)
- ❌ Feet not flat on ground (circulation issues)
- ❌ Screen too close (<20 inches)

### Setup Mistakes
- ❌ Buying chair without testing (most returned item)
- ❌ Insufficient desk depth (<24 inches)
- ❌ Ignoring cable management (visual stress)
- ❌ Overhead lighting only (screen glare)
- ❌ Back to window (backlighting strain)

### Investment Errors
- ❌ Spending on aesthetics over ergonomics
- ❌ Buying cheap chair (false economy)
- ❌ Skimping on monitor quality (8+ hours/day use)
- ❌ Not testing standing desk motors (weight capacity)
