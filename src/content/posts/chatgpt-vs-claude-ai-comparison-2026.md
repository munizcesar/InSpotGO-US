## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use multiple AI assistants?**
A: Yes, many professionals use ChatGPT for creative tasks, Claude for analysis, and Gemini for Google Workspace integration. Free tiers allow testing before committing.

**Q: Which is best for students?**
A: Claude for learning (better explanations, lower error rate). ChatGPT for projects requiring creativity. Both offer free tiers suitable for students.

**Q: Are conversations private?**
A: All platforms have data retention policies. Claude Pro/Enterprise offers strongest privacy. ChatGPT Plus allows opt-out of training. Gemini integrates with Google account (privacy tradeoff).

**Q: Which has the best mobile app?**
A: ChatGPT offers the most polished mobile experience with voice mode. Gemini integrates well on Android devices. Claude's mobile app is functional but basic.

**Q: Can these replace Google Search?**
A: Not entirely. Gemini combines AI with Search effectively. ChatGPT and Claude work better for analysis than real-time information. Use Search for latest news, prices, local information.

**Q: Which is best for non-English languages?**
A: ChatGPT and Gemini support 50+ languages well. Claude is primarily optimized for English with limited multilingual support.
