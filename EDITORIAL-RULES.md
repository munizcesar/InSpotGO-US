# 📋 InSpotGO-US — Editorial Rules

> These rules apply to ALL content published on InSpotGO-US. Every writer, editor, and AI assistant must follow them without exception.

---

## 🎯 Content Strategy

### 1. SaaS & Software First
The primary focus is **SaaS and Software tools with recurring affiliate commissions**. Tech hardware reviews are secondary and complementary. Always prioritize tools that pay **monthly/annual recurring commissions** — this is the financial backbone of the site.

### 2. Evergreen Content Only
All content must be **timeless and price-free**. Write about benefits, use cases, workflow impact, and daily-life applications — not prices, tiers, or promotional values.

**❌ NEVER write:**
- "The Basic plan costs $X/month"
- "At $12/seat, it's great value"
- "Pricing starts at $9/mo"
- "On the $19 Pro plan you get..."
- Any specific dollar amounts for plans or products

**✅ ALWAYS write:**
- "Monday.com offers plans for individuals, teams, and enterprises"
- "Higher-tier plans unlock automations and advanced dashboards"
- "Pricing scales with team size — check the official site for current offers"
- "There's a free plan available to get started"

### 3. Sell the Benefit, Not the Price
The reader should want the product because of **what it does for their life or work** — not because it's cheap. Lead with:
- Real-world scenarios and daily use cases
- Time saved, problems solved, workflows improved
- Who the tool is ideal for
- Practical advantages over alternatives

---

## 🔗 Affiliate Strategy

### 4. Direct Affiliate Links — Skip the Marketplace
Always try to join affiliate programs **directly** through the SaaS company's website (usually in their footer under "Affiliates" or "Partners"). Many use PartnerStack behind the scenes but allow direct sign-ups without Marketplace approval. This bypasses platform-level gatekeeping.

### 5. Recurring Commissions Priority
When choosing which tools to promote, always prioritize programs that offer **recurring commissions** (paid monthly as long as the customer stays subscribed). One-time commissions are secondary.

---

## ✍️ Writing Style

- Write in **English**, targeting a US and global English-speaking audience
- Tone: helpful, professional, direct — like a knowledgeable friend, not a salesperson
- No hype, no clickbait, no urgency tricks
- Every claim must be backed by real usage, not speculation
- Keep FAQs price-free — redirect to the official site for current pricing

---

## 🔄 Content Review

All existing posts must be audited against these rules. Any post containing specific prices must be updated to remove them and reframe around benefits.
